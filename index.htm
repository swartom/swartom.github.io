PhD student in the Networking Lab at Lancaster

I am interested in random graphs which have formalised constraints, mainly in the context of something called a planted problem. An example of a well-studied planted problem is a planted clique. I try to focus on the generation of these problems from string languages. The research question mainly regards test oracles for graph-based problems (An example of such a problem is routing). So we may generate extremely large and diverse data-sets for algorithm analysis.

If I publish anything, there will be copies here.
