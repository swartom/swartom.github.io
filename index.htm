Hello! Welcome!

I am a PhD student in the Networking Lab at Lancaster University, Where you can find me researching and teaching about softwarised networks and Data-structures.

I research data-structures under real-world hardware enforced contraints. Literally, how to fit a square peg into a round hole. I focus on Graphs: generating and modifying them across distributed compute using rewriting. I think about how graphs transit network infrastructure, then how the data-plane can change them.
